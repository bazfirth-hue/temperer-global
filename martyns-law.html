Deciphering Section 27 Statutory Guidance

On April 15, 2026, the Home Office formally issued the official Section 27 statutory guidance framework. This critical release shifts Martyn's Law from a theoretical protective measure into an active, enforceable legal countdown. UK businesses operating publicly accessible spaces have a strict 24-month implementation period to evaluate, draft, and deploy compliant security systems before facing severe regulatory sanctions overseen by the Security Industry Authority (SIA).

"Compliance is not determined by client averages; it is assessed entirely against your peak capacity threshold, explicitly including all on-site personnel, operational contractors, and active staff members."
